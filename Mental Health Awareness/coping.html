Coping Mechanisms

Therapy

Therapy can provide a safe space to explore thoughts and emotions, offering tools for managing mental health challenges.

Mindfulness

Mindfulness practices help reduce stress and improve focus, bringing awareness to the present moment.

Journaling

Journaling is a powerful tool to process emotions and reflect on thoughts, helping individuals to track their mental health journey.

Physical Exercise

Exercise helps boost mood, reduce anxiety, and increase energy levels. It is a great way to improve overall well-being and mental clarity.
